# **Solo Chroma Build**

Chroma is the best option for solo credit farming.

CHROMA BUILD PIC

2 Tau Energy on start shards provide enough energy for the entire run. You only need to upkeep Vex Armour and Shock, Elemental Ward is not required. Runnning a Decaying Dragon Key lets you stack Vex Armour faster after landing, however you can also just cast Void Strike if running Madurai.

OGRIS BUILD

Overcharge will proc from your Archwing's energy, make sure you stay in the air until your final shot lands to benefit from the Vengeful Archwing intrinsic.

CORVAS BUILD

Corvas is used on Necramech. Mausolon no longer has an advantage as Corvas can now oneshot legs without a riven. Make sure not to spam abilities in mech so Primary Overcharge stays active.

PANZER VULPAPHYLA 

Panzer is the ideal beast companion as it has good enough base stats to activate all relevant bond mods without conditionals and can slot Prosperous Retriever.

Magus Elevate provides survivability during the run. Use Itzal with Hyperion Thrusters and Auxiliary Power.
